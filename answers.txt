Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Git is a useful tool when collaborating with others on projects.

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: The difference between merging and rebasing is that merging is a safe option that preserves the history of your repository where rebasing creates a linear history by moving your feature branch onto the tip of main.
